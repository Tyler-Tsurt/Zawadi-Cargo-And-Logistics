Our Story

Zambian-Owned.
World-Class Service.

Zawadi Cargo and Logistics Limited is a proudly Zambian-owned Customs Clearing & Forwarding Agent, established in 2012 with its head office in Lusaka. Over the years we have grown to serve clients across Zambia and internationally with specialised freight and clearance solutions.

We act as a single point of contact for all parties involved — arranging Shipping, Clearance & Forwarding to create the most efficient avenue for your freight and logistical requirements.

VisionA leading contributor to national development in the clearing services sector.
